**The Prime Modality: Architects of Perception (Pd1-Pd3)**

The Prime Modality, comprising the Psycho-Volitional Dimension (Pd1), the Psycho-Conceptive Dimension (Pd2), and the Psycho-Meditative Dimension (Pd3), is the cognitive-executive triad governing our initial perception, intention, and interpretation.
